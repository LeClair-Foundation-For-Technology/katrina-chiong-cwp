CVS QuickServe Cartridge Redesign

Marks Design Co-Op 2023

The CVS QuickServe Dispensing System is a vitamin-dispensing system that utilizes disposable vitamin refill cartridges. The goal of this project was to modify the existing cartridge design to be refillable and to find alternative refill methods that can result in reduced plastic usage. To create the final concepts, I went through multiple stages of sketching and rapid prototyping and used SolidWorks and Keyshot.
